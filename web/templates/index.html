Trust-Based Recommendations

What are Trust Scores?

Our recommendations come with transparent trust scores that show you exactly why a college is recommended for you. Each score is based on multiple factors:

  • Academic Profile Match
  • Emotional Readiness
  • College Fit
  • Budget Alignment

You can see the breakdown of each factor and understand the reasoning behind every recommendation.

Emotion-Aware Counseling

Why Emotions Matter

College decisions shouldn't be made in moments of high stress or anxiety. Our system monitors your emotional state through your journal entries and helps you make decisions when you're in the right frame of mind.

If we detect high levels of stress or anxiety, we'll suggest taking a break before making important decisions.

Your emotional well-being is just as important as finding the right academic fit.

Override System

Disagree with a recommendation? Our override system allows you to adjust recommendations with proper justification, maintaining transparency throughout the process.
