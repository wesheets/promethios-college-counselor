About Journaling

Your journal entries help our system understand your emotional state and provide better college recommendations. We analyze your entries to detect:

  • Sentiment: How positive or negative your feelings are
  • Certainty: How confident you feel about your decisions
  • Calmness: How stressed or relaxed you are about the process

This emotional awareness helps us tailor our recommendations to your current state of mind and provide guidance that's appropriate for where you are in your journey.

Your journal entries are private and only used to improve your recommendations.
